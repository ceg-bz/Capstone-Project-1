Hobbies

My Hobbies include:

Fishing

It allows me to unplug from my daily routine and reconnect with nature. It has assisted me greatly in learning patience.


Swimming

Swimming is a great way for me to stay fit while also being refreshing and fun. Growing up in a small town with the shore at a stones throw away, really created the perfect storm for me to be a fish out of water.


Basketball

Basketball keeps me active and helps me improve teamwork and coordination. I love the fast-paced nature of the game and the strategy involved in both offense and defense. Playing with friends makes it even more enjoyable.


Learning New Skills

I am passionate about continuous learning and enjoy picking up new skills. Whether it's coding, reading or picking up a new hobby, I find personal growth and excitement in expanding my knowledge. Learning keeps me motivated and adaptable.
